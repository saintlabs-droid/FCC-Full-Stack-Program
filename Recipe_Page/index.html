Chicken Pizza Recipe

Here's a delicious homemade chicken pizza recipe you can try:

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast (shredded or diced)
  • ½ cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup sliced onions
  • ½ cup sliced bell peppers (red, green, or yellow)
  • ¼ cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil. Knead until smooth (about 8–10 minutes).
  3. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  4. Season cooked chicken with salt, pepper, and a pinch of paprika or Italian seasoning for extra flavor.
  5. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  6. Roll out the dough into a 12-inch circle and place on a greased baking tray or pizza stone.
  7. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  8. Add chicken, onions, bell peppers, olives, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes on top.
  10. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  11. Top with fresh basil leaves, slice, and enjoy!
